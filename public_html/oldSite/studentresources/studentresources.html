Student Responsibilities

  1. Cooperate during practice and lessons
  2. Quietly wash hands, trim nails, go to the bathroom and get your guitar and materials ready before each lesson
  3. Treat parents, teachers, and other students with respect
  4. Listen to the recording daily
  5. Have PRACTICE LOG filled in before each lesson.
